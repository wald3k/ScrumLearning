Welcome to SCRUM E-Learning system

How it actually works?
Our new SCRUM E-Learning system allows you to not only learn both SCRUM methodology but also brush-up your programming & communication skills!
It will allow you to step through a whole Sprint and prepare a piece of code that can actually be shipped or further developed!
Select one of avaliable courses, such as:
  • Snake game
  • Asteroids
  • Space invaders
Where to start?
First learn the material on a webpage. It is easily accessible as it is in .PDF format. Then you can test your knowledge by solving a specially prepared QUIZ. After successfully passing our QUIZ, user will be allowed to preceed with the Scrum E-Learing course.
Let's start the project
Starting a new project is an exciting activity! Our Scrum E-Learing system gives you a great amount of flexibility and tries to simulate real-life problems. That's why you start with assigning scrum-roles to people that are involved in the project!

Decide which stories should be included in the project

Use Sticker functionality to move user-stories to different sections in product backlog. This step is very intuitive and fun. Just drag user story that you want to its destination place inside product backlog. This step will determine how your project will be evolving. After stories are properly distributed save your progress and proceed to the Sprint!
Scrum in action - your first sprint
Sprint is one of the most important Scrum steps. It is a time period during which team can work on their product. The goal is to be able to present some working part of the code after this time. During this time team members pick stories that they want to work on, so that they can work independently.
Summarize your Scrum project
Did you succeed in creating your scrum project? Now it is a good time to analyze the results that you've achieved. Analyze percentage of course realized, how well the user stories were organized. Leave your comments of what could be done better!
